About Me

Hi, I'm Chandrashekhar! I'm an engineer and researcher passionate about creating smart, miniaturized systems — from sensors and MEMS devices to advanced prototypes. I'm fascinated by how things are designed and built, from microfabrication and precision engineering to scaling ideas into real-world solutions.
